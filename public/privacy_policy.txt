VAGUDLE PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: May 2026

Vagudle (https://vagudle.king-tajin.dev) is operated by Roland Shikoski.

For questions or data requests contact:
  developer@king-tajin.dev
  support@king-tajin.dev

---

LOCAL STORAGE

Your game state, settings, and progress are stored locally in your browser
using localStorage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible
to us.

---

DUEL RESULTS

When you complete a duel, the following data is stored in a database to power
the leaderboard:

  - Your Discord ID
  - Whether you won or lost
  - The number of guesses used
  - The word used in the duel
  - The timestamp of completion

This data is retained indefinitely. To request deletion of your data, contact
us at the email addresses above.

---

ANALYTICS

This site uses Cloudflare Web Analytics, which collects anonymous, aggregated
traffic data. It does not use cookies, does not track you across sites, and
does not collect any personal information. For more information see
https://www.cloudflare.com/web-analytics/

---

COOKIES

Vagudle does not use cookies.

---

YOUR RIGHTS

If you are located in the European Union or United Kingdom, you have the right
to access, correct, or request deletion of any personal data we hold about
you. To exercise these rights, contact us at the email addresses above.

If you are located in California, you have rights under the California
Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). We do not sell your personal data.

---

CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this location
with a revised date at the top.
