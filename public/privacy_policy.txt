VAGUDLE PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: July 2026

Vagudle (https://vagudle.king-tajin.dev) is operated by Roland Shikoski.

For questions or data requests contact:
  developer@king-tajin.dev
  support@king-tajin.dev

---

LOCAL STORAGE

Your game state, settings, and progress are stored locally in your browser
using localStorage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible
to us, unless you choose to sign in for cloud save as described below.

---

CLOUD SAVE ACCOUNTS

Signing in is optional. If you choose to sign in, you can sync your
achievements, statistics, settings, and selected background across devices.

Sign-in is handled by Firebase Authentication, a service provided by Google.
You can sign in with:

  - Google
  - GitHub
  - A sign-in link sent to your email address

When you sign in, we store the following in a database to power cloud save:

  - Your account identifier, issued by Firebase
  - Your email address and/or display name, depending on the sign-in method
    you use
  - Your achievement progress, word statistics, settings, and selected
    background

This data is retained until you request deletion. To request deletion of
your cloud save or account data, contact us at the email addresses above.

Your browser may also store a local sign-in session (using localStorage or
IndexedDB, not cookies) so you stay signed in between visits. If you sign
in with Google or GitHub, that sign-in is also subject to the provider's own
privacy policy. We never receive your password from these providers.

---

DUEL RESULTS

When you complete a duel, the following data is stored in a database to power
the leaderboard:

  - Your Discord ID
  - Whether you won or lost
  - The number of guesses used
  - The word used in the duel
  - The timestamp of completion

This data is retained indefinitely. To request deletion of your data, contact
us at the email addresses above.

---

FEEDBACK

When you submit feedback through the in-app feedback form, the following
data is stored:

  - The feedback message, category, and sentiment you provide
  - Your email address, if you choose to provide one
  - Your IP address and browser user agent
  - The timestamp of submission

This data is retained indefinitely and used only to review and respond to
feedback. To request deletion of your data, contact us at the email
addresses above.

---

ANALYTICS

This site uses Cloudflare Web Analytics, which collects anonymous, aggregated
traffic data. It does not use cookies, does not track you across sites, and
does not collect any personal information. For more information see
https://www.cloudflare.com/web-analytics/

---

COOKIES

Vagudle does not use cookies.

---

YOUR RIGHTS

If you are located in the European Union or United Kingdom, you have the right
to access, correct, or request deletion of any personal data we hold about
you. To exercise these rights, contact us at the email addresses above.

If you are located in California, you have rights under the California
Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). We do not sell your personal data.
