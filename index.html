Vagudle: The Free Word Guessing Game

Vagudle is a free word-guessing game inspired by Hardle, with extra tools to help you solve the puzzle and no pesky daily limit to get in your way. Built and maintained by King-Tajin.

How to Play

Type a word and press Enter to submit a guess. You have 11 tries to find the hidden word. Select a brush, then click or drag cells to mark what you know: green for the right letter in the right spot, yellow for the right letter in the wrong spot, and gray for letters not in the word.

Features

Variable Word Length

Play with anywhere between 4 and 7-letter words via Settings.

Hard Mode

Solutions are selected from uncommon words and the player is limited to 9 guesses.

Cell Painting

Select a brush and click or drag across cells to color them.

Auto-Gray

Automatically grays out letters from fully-gray rows.

Auto-Green

Fills in user marked correct letters across all rows automatically.

Custom Challenges

Open Settings and go to the Challenge tab. Pick a dictionary, choose how many guesses to allow, type your secret word, and hit Generate Link. Share the link to let others play your custom word with exactly the settings you chose. Results never count toward the recipient's stats.

Discord Integration

Use the /vagudle_challenge slash command to generate a challenge link directly from Discord, or compete in head-to-head Duels on a live leaderboard.

Open Source

Vagudle is open source on GitHub and based on react-wordle. Contributions and feedback are welcome.
